Privacy Policy

SubFinder is a private, invite-only messaging service operated by Comeback Gear LLC. We process limited personal data (name, mobile number, role/availability) to notify opted-in substitute teachers about open classroom coverage. We do not sell personal data or use it for marketing.

Data we collect

  • Contact info provided by you or your school (name, phone number, role/availability).
  • Messaging metadata (timestamps, delivery/response status).

How we use data

  • Send and manage operational SMS relating to substitute opportunities.
  • Maintain records for compliance and troubleshooting.

Opt-in / Opt-out

Participation is optional and by consent. Reply STOP to end messages. Reply HELP for help. Message & data rates may apply.

Sharing

We share data only with service providers necessary to deliver SMS (e.g., messaging carriers) and as required by law.

Security

We use commercially reasonable safeguards. No method is 100% secure; please contact us if you suspect unauthorized use.
